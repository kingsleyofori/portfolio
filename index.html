About Me

Hello, I'm Kingsley, a self-taught data analyst who likes the rush that comes from figuring out challenging problems. During my undergraduate studies, I was introduced to financial modeling and case study analysis, which sparked my interest in data analytics. As it turns out, employing all those tactics and tips helped me learn a lot about Excel, Stata, MySQL, R-software, and Power BI.

An organized individual, problem-solver, and independent worker who pays close attention to detail. Fan of the 'Suit' TV series, outdoor activities, and Robert Greene's books. I constantly strive to produce the greatest results on any projects I take on since I'm diligent, and result-oriented. Do you want us to work together? Please shoot me an email.
